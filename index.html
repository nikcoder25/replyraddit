Frequently asked questions

Do I have to connect my Reddit account?

No. ReplyRaddit only reads public Reddit content to find opportunities. You never link your account or share credentials, which keeps your account safe.

Does it post replies automatically?

Never. We generate drafts for you to review. You decide what to edit and what to post manually, keeping you fully compliant with Reddit's terms.

How does the relevance scoring work?

Each post is evaluated across multiple factors — topic relevance carries the most weight — so the highest-intent conversations rise to the top of your dashboard.

Will the replies sound like marketing?

No. Drafts are built from your brand and persona to be genuinely helpful and value-first. You can adjust tone and regenerate until it sounds like you.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month and you can cancel whenever you like. Lifetime deals are a one-time payment with no recurring fees.
